BIG BANG - Type Experiment

With total creative freedom and no rules to follow, I dove into typographic and illustrative experiments around the concept of the “Big Bang.” I started by playing with a wide range of visual ideas, freely exploring form, texture, and type. From there, I picked my favorite designs and began combining and refining them further. In the end, I developed the two strongest concepts into bold, expressive posters that capture the explosive energy of the theme.
