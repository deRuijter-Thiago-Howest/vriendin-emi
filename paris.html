Paris Colis – inspired by French Bread Studio

For this project, the goal was to create a fictional design package based on the visual style and working method of an existing design studio. I chose French Bread Studio, a creative collective known for their playful, expressive work and strong use of typography, color, and composition.

Rather than copying their work, the task was to interpret their style and translate it into a new, personal concept that reflects their spirit and design approach.

The result is Paris Colis — a curated package containing a custom-designed box filled with various printed and designed items:

  • 3 postcards
  • 1 magazine
  • a flag
  • and 2 custom-made gadgets

Each item was designed to reflect the bold, experimental nature of French Bread Studio, while still bringing in my own design voice. The focus was on creating a cohesive visual identity that feels dynamic, fresh, and rooted in contemporary graphic culture — much like the work of the studio that inspired it.
