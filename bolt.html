BOLT - Hotel Branding & Webdesign

This project revolved around a unique hotel concept designed for makers, tinkerers, and hands-on thinkers — a hotel for people who find joy in doing things themselves. The DIY Hotel isn’t your typical overnight stay. Guests don’t just check in; they get involved. From assembling their own bed to picking the wall finishes, they actively shape their own experience. It’s a place where creativity, effort, and pride in making come together.

The website mirrors the hotel’s physical experience: interactive, characterful, and no-nonsense. From modular layout structures to tactile-feeling micro-interactions, every element was crafted to reflect the brand’s core values — making, trying, adjusting, and embracing imperfection. It’s designed to feel like something you could almost build yourself.

Visually, the brand identity is bold, honest, and a little rough around the edges — just like the hotel. The design language balances structure with spontaneity: clean but playful type, an earthy yet energetic color palette, and a sense of order that always leaves room for a quirk or two. Everything is aligned… with one corner just slightly off — on purpose.

This wasn’t just about building a website. It was about crafting an experience for people who take pride in doing it themselves — down to the last screw.
