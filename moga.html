MOGA festival – Font, Vinyl & Poster

For this project, our group designed a visual identity inspired by the MOGA Festival in Essaouira, Morocco — a boutique electronic music festival that blends global sounds with local traditions. Together, we developed a custom typeface rooted in Amazigh (Berber) symbols and Arabic calligraphic influences. The letterforms are modular and experimental, reflecting the fusion of Moroccan visual heritage with the futuristic character of electronic music.

Building on this typographic foundation, I created a poster and vinyl packaging that extend the same conceptual contrast. The poster combines hypnotic, wave-like patterns with bold symbolic letterforms to capture both the ritualistic and energetic atmosphere of the festival. The vinyl label design features fragmented, layered typography, evoking the texture and rhythm of a live electronic set. For the innersleeve, I used a soft blue-to-purple gradient to represent the sunset on Essaouira’s Atlantic coast — a calm, atmospheric moment that mirrors the emotional arc of the music.

The result is a visual system that merges the authentic and the digital, the handcrafted and the immersive — echoing the unique cultural crossover that defines MOGA Festival.
