Portfolio About

Hi, I’m Maren Van de Voorde — a graphic designer with a love for bold visuals, clear ideas, and the subtle details that bring design to life.

What draws me to design is its ability to say a lot without using words. A well-crafted layout, the right typeface, a carefully chosen color — these elements can shape how we see a brand, how we feel about a message, or how we remember a moment. That’s what excites me: creating visuals that not only look good, but feel right.

My work focuses on visual identity and printed design — think posters, invitations, logos, and brand materials. I enjoy the process of translating ideas into strong, visual systems that feel personal and intentional. I’m not into loud trends or fast solutions; I’d rather create something that lasts, that fits, and that tells your story in an honest way.

As a designer, I like to keep things clear, but never boring. I believe in design that’s direct, but full of character. I enjoy working with people who care about the details, who are open to fresh ideas, and who want design that feels true to who they are.

Based in Wingene, I’m always open to new collaborations — whether it’s a small personal project or a full visual identity. Let’s make something worth looking at twice.
