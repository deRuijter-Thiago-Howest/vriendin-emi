Taart - Magazine

For this project, we designed a magazine around a single object — we chose cake. Cake is more than just a dessert; it’s a symbol of warmth, comfort, and togetherness. It reminds us of birthdays, family gatherings, and cozy afternoons. Through playful layouts, soft colors, and inviting photography, we aimed to capture that familiar, feel-good atmosphere. Our goal was to make readers feel like they were stepping into a sweet, nostalgic moment with every page.
